Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our objective for each student entrusted to the educators at Omeed Private High School is to help them become individuals who are ready for future, with the knowledge and abilities to compete with or lead their peers throughout the world.

Our Mission

To provide a curriculum that is broad, balanced, coherent, and relevant To assist students in appreciating human achievement in all forms: art, literature, science, technology, and physical pursuits and in encouraging participation in these fields as appropriate To help them acquire the knowledge and skills that will enable them to lead fulfilling lives at School, home, and work, through academic and personal development opportunities. To foster spiritual and personal development and a sense of social responsibility. To promote tolerance in diversity. To enable the students to gain the wisdom and accept the people of other races, nationalities and their beliefs, cultures, and way of life encouraging them to live in harmony. To develop the care and concern for others in all pupils.
