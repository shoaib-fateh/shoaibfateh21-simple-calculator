Founder ‘s Message

“

Omeed Private High School provides academically demanding and fulfilling worldwide programs that focus on the development of individual abilities as well as intellectual, social, and emotional growth as the motto goes, Omeed Private High School Educates the Future.

Omeed School strives to cultivate enquiring, informed, and self-assured lifelong learners who are honest and endowed with universal principles and intercultural understanding in order to build a peaceful and diverse Afghanistan via a student-centered and balanced education.

We encourage students to be creative and socially active multilingual learners who are responsible global citizens. We encourage students to build their own national and spiritual ideals, as well as courteous students who recognize and believe that other individuals have different values and points of view.

We provide students the opportunity to grow, learn, and enjoy an environment conducive to fostering academic growth. The experience obtained at Omeed Private High School is unique and helps to drive the intellectual, academic, social, and athletic success of the students.

With our inclusive and committed staff, we are here for you working hard to help pen-pushers write for the future on their own. We firmly believe that today’s learners are tomorrow’s leaders, challengers, innovators, and pioneers. Best wishes Masood Wardak Founder of Omeed Private High School

”
